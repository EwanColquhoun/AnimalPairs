Welcome to Animal Pairs!

How to Play

The aim of the game is to match all the cards.
You can select your level of difficulty using the difficulty buttons.
When you select a card, it turns over to reveal an animal.
Select another card to see if the new animal matches the one already shown.
When you have successfully matched all the pairs your score and time will be displayed.
You will then have the option to compete again at a level of your choice!

Choose a difficulty to begin!
